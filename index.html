About

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Applied Mathematics at Example University, where I lead the Distributed-Parameter Control group. My research develops rigorous control methods for systems described by partial differential equations, with a focus on boundary actuation and the gap between finite- and infinite-dimensional theory.

I received my Ph.D. in Control Engineering, defended with distinction, on the boundary control of partial differential equations. I collaborate across Europe and Asia on energy systems, mobility, and data-driven control.
